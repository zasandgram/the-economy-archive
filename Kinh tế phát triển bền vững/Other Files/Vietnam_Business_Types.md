## Comparison of Key Business Types

| Feature | Limited Liability Company (LLC) | Joint Stock Company (JSC) | Private Enterprise (Sole Proprietorship) | Partnership | Representative Office (RO) |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Ownership** | 1 to 50 members (Single or Multi-member). | Minimum 3 shareholders. No limit. | 1 Individual. | At least 2 General Partners. | Parent Company. |
| **Liability** | Limited to capital contribution. | Limited to share value. | Unlimited liability (owner's assets). | Unlimited for General Partners. | Liability rests with Parent Company. |
| **Capital** | Contributed capital. No shares. | Shares. Can issue stocks/bonds. | Owner's capital. No securities. | No securities. | N/A (Funded by Parent). |
| **Public Listing** | No. | Yes (Stock Exchange). | No. | No. | No. |
| **Operational Scope** | Full commercial activities. | Full commercial activities. | Full commercial activities. | Full commercial activities. | Non-profit generating (Liaison/Market Research only). |
| **Legal Status** | Legal entity. | Legal entity. | No separate legal status. | Legal entity. | Dependent unit. |

## Detailed Breakdown

### 1. Limited Liability Company (LLC)
*   **Best for:** SMEs, foreign investors wanting full control (Single Member) or joint ventures (Multi-member).
*   **Pros:** Limited liability, simple structure, fewer compliance requirements than JSC.
*   **Cons:** Cannot issue shares/go public.

### 2. Joint Stock Company (JSC)
*   **Best for:** Medium to large enterprises, companies planning to IPO.
*   **Pros:** Ability to raise large capital via shares, public listing allowed.
*   **Cons:** Complex management structure, strict reporting/compliance.

### 3. Private Enterprise
*   **Best for:** Small individual businesses.
*   **Pros:** Total control, simple setup.
*   **Cons:** Unlimited liability is a major risk.

### 4. Representative Office
*   **Best for:** Foreign companies testing the market.
*   **Pros:** Low cost, easy to setup.
*   **Cons:** Cannot earn revenue/profit in Vietnam.
